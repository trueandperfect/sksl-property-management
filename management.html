Comprehensive Management Services

01

Comprehensive Tenant Screening & Placement

Finding the right tenant is the single most important factor in successful real estate management. Our screening is thorough and compliant with all Massachusetts fair housing laws.

  • Credit history and financial verification
  • Employment and income confirmation
  • Previous landlord references
  • Criminal background checks
  • Eviction history review
02

Proactive Maintenance & Property Care

Deferred maintenance is the silent killer of property value. We maintain a vetted network of licensed contractors, plumbers, electricians, and maintenance professionals across Massachusetts.

  • 24/7 emergency response line for tenants
  • Vetted, licensed contractor network statewide
  • Scheduling, supervision, and quality verification
  • Proactive seasonal inspections
03

Rent Collection & Financial Reporting

We enforce lease terms consistently and offer tenants convenient online payment options. Every owner receives detailed monthly statements and year-end tax-ready documentation including 1099 forms.

  • Online payment options for tenants
  • Structured late-payment follow-up
  • Detailed monthly income and expense statements
  • Year-end 1099 and categorized expense reports
04

Legal Compliance & Risk Mitigation

Massachusetts has some of the most tenant-protective laws in the country. Our team stays current on every change to state and local housing laws, handling lease preparation, renewals, and when necessary, the eviction process from start to finish.

  • Security deposit regulations & compliance
  • Lead paint disclosure requirements
  • Lease preparation and renewal negotiations
  • End-to-end eviction management when needed
05

Market Analysis & Rental Pricing

We continuously monitor Massachusetts rental market conditions to ensure your property is priced competitively. Our data-driven approach minimizes vacancy periods while maximizing your rental income over the long term.

  • Comparable property analysis
  • Vacancy rate monitoring
  • Seasonal demand pattern analysis
  • Custom pricing recommendations per property
